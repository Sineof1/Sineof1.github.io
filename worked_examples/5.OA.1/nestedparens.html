Simplify Numerical Expressions with Nested Parentheses

Simplify the expression \(\small\mathtt{5 + (2 \times (4 - 3))^2}\).

The expression inside the innermost parentheses is simplified first.

\(\small\mathtt{5 + (2 \times \color{red}{(4 - 3)})^2 \rightarrow 5 + (2 \times \color{red}{1})^2}\)

The expression in the outer parentheses is simplified next.

\(\small\mathtt{5 + \color{red}{(2 \times 1)}^2 \rightarrow 5 + \color{red}{2}^2}\)
\(\small\mathtt{5 + 2^2 \rightarrow 5 + 4}\), or 9.
